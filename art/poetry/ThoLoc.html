Abstract My poems, which would rather lean forward melody than lean forward prosody, are half popular half academic, half elegant half vulgar, half deep half humorous, half queer half naive, half modern half ancient. There are mundane men with many careers, as well as many fairies, ghosts, heroes, nymphs in my poems. There are also tears, smiles, unreal stories, hot news and many things. I see myself in my poems and I will be very glad if catching you in my poems.
