Loc Nguyen, Bich-Ngoc Tran

This Chinese vocabulary book contains common Chinese words along with short conversations and stories. Main contents of the book are extracted from text books such as Primary Chinese Textbooks, BOYA, and HSK by authors Hieu-Ky Ly, Tuyet-Mai Nham, Xuong-Nghi Du, Le-Binh Khuong, Dieu Vu, Lam Ly, Thu-Ha Nguyen, and Minh-Hong T. Nguyen. Main referred dictionaries are Vietnamese Dictionary (vdict.co), LINE (ce.linedict.com), Chinese-Vietnamese Dictionary (Quynh-Hoa, Minh-Vu).

This book is editing. The preprint is available at https://osf.io/sqndv
